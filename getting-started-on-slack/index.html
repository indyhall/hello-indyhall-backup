Getting Started on Slack

Joining Slack can be a bit overwhelming- you’ve got your email invitation from Sam or Adam, maybe you’ve gotten your account set up, and now you’re looking at a LOT of channels… so what do you do next?

Well first, did you know that Slack has a user guide to help you get started? It’s a really great guide to using all its features, customizing your preferences, and even adding custom emoji. Check it out!

The easiest way to use slack is with the app- it’s free, and allows you to access a bunch of cool features, like combining your teams into one window for easy access to both. If you want to keep the conversation going while you’re on the go, you can also download slack on your smart phone!

Channels at Indy Hall

Indy Hall has, at the time I’m typing this, 89 channels. That is a lot, and way more than anyone can dive into. To help you find the conversations you want to be part of, check out this handy guide of channels at Indy Hall!

Indy Hall Essentials

#general– it’s the first channel you’re sent to when you join Slack, and it has everything you need to know, including cross-posts from Groupbuzz and Instagram. This is where you’ll find reminders about events going on, conversations that include the whole community, and everything that doesn’t really fit in another channel.

#indyskillz– Do you want to learn a new skill? Do you have something you’re dying to teach? Awesome member Lydia runs #indyskillz, a channel where you can set up skill shares or just ask a quick question to get help with something.

#daily-goals– post 3 (or more) CRISPY things you’re getting done today- it will keep you on track, and you’ll find “accountabilibuddies” to ask questions and see how what you’re working on is progressing!

#lunchroom– Everybody has to eat, so why not eat with other Hallers? Jump on the #lunchroom channel to see if anyone wants to grab a bite with you, or to get recommendations of places around Indy Hall to check out.

#happy-hour– Wanna grab a drink after work with your coworkers? This is where members plan happy hours, as well as discuss deals around town. Want to talk more specifically about beer? Check out the #beer channel.

#overheardatthehall– Did you hear something silly around Indy Hall, especially taken out of context? Log it here… but no attributing! All of the quotes here also go up on the wall in the kitchen.

#gig-swap– Have a job opening? Looking for work? This is where all the job postings live at Indy Hall.

Beyond the Essentials, there’s a lot more to check out!

I’ve broken our 80+ channels into categories, so you can easily find where you want to jump in! Not every channel is active all the time (that would be a lot of chatter!), but it’s worth popping into any channels that appeal to you, to see who you meet.

Let’s Get Productive!

#daily-goals– post 3 (or more) CRISPY things you’re getting done today- it will keep you on track, and you’ll find “accountabilibuddies” to ask questions and see how what you’re working on is progressing!

#third-eye– need another set of eyes on something you’re working on? Hop on #third-eye to share and take a look at what everyone else is working on.

#gig-swap– Have a job opening? Looking for work? This is where all the job postings live at Indy Hall.

#devs– are you a developer? Whether you’re new or very experienced, this is the place for you!

#design– is design your jam? Hop into the design channel to share articles and talk shop!

#front-end-dev– If you’re a front-end developer, this is the channel for you!

#writersclubhouse– Whether you’re a professional writer or you just write for fun, this is your spot to talk about your process, the business, share what you’re working on, or grab a happy hour with your fellow writers.

Wanna Talk Pop Culture?

#music– Whether you love rock, hip hop, jazz, experimental, classic… #music has everything.

#movies– Did you just see a great movie? Is there one you’re looking forward to, or one you’d like to see with other members? This is the place to discuss all things movies.

#podcasts– We don’t only talk about Serial! However, if you’re hooked on a podcast, looking for a recommendation, or, yes, want to talk about Serial, this is the place for you. 

#humor–Do you love improv, sketch, standup, or silly internet videos? This channel is for you! Even you, punster!

#bookworms– Welcome, readers! This is the spot to share what you’re reading, get recommendations, and talk about authors!

#bteam– Want to talk “dumb nerd stuff?” Do you wait with bated breath for the next issue of your favorite comic book or Marvel movie? Let’s talk. 

#video-games– What are you playing right now? What are you excited to play? Need recommendations? This is the spot for you.

#sports– Whether you want to talk about last night’s game or plan where to watch the next one together, this is the place!

Let’s Go Do Stuff!

#nightowls– are you coming in to work on Tuesday night? Find out who will be there, plan to order food together, or plan other activities for Tuesday nights!

#happy-hour– find a time to grab a beer with your Indy Hall buddies! If you don’t know anyone at Indy Hall yet, this is also a great place to ask if anyone wants to grab a drink with you- it’s a great way to meet people!

#lunchroom– You have to eat, so why not grab food with other Hallers? This is a great spot to plan to get lunch with people, or ask for recommendations in the area!

#boardgames– There are a lot of board game enthusiasts at Indy Hall- if you want to talk about games you play (or are thinking about playing), or join in for a board game night, this is for you!

#kickbawl– Wanna play some kickball? Spring through the early fall, members have played kickball together on weekends. We’re pretty okay at it, and not very competitive- if you want to join next season, hop into this channel!

#chikaratopia– Do you love pro wrestling? Maybe you’ve always wanted to try it, or see it in person… well most of the members in this channel feel the same way! This is the group to organize seeing wrestling events together, or talk about the latest match. Not sure what CHIKARA is? Talk to Adam!

#alpine-start– Do you love rock climbing, hiking, or other outdoor adventures? This is the conversation for you! Members use this channel to plan trips to get out and get into nature!

#fantasy-football– If you love playing fantasy football, and want to join the Indy Hall league, pop into this channel.

#quizzo– Do you love quizzo, but need a good spot to play, or some people to join your team? This is where you’ll find them!

Looking for resources at the Hall?

#indyhallops– This is the channel for “taking care of this place”– whether you’re looking for more paper towels,  or have a question about why something is the way it  at Indy Hall, this is the channel to talk about it.

#indyslist– This channel is for looking for and recommending local services- Indy Hall members love giving recommendations and sharing their experiences, so if you have a question, please join the conversation!

#indyskillz- Want to learn a new skill? Know something cool you want to teach someone? Have a quick question about something to ask the community? This is the place!

#practicar-espanol– Quieres practicar Español? Join other Spanish-learning members to practice your language skills!

#ih-darkroom– Did you know Indy Hall has a darkroom? If you want to use it to screen print or develop photos, this is the channel to connect you with the members that can show you how!

#for-sale– Whether you’re looking to get rid of something, or looking for something in particular, this is a good channel to keep an eye on.

Hobbies and Interests

#clambake– Don’t be distracted by the channel name- this channel is for ladies and LGBTQ-folk and the people that love them.

#indyhall-arts– Are you interested in getting involved with the arts at Indy Hall? Wanna share your work, or help plan the next show? This channel is for you!

#parentsdontunderstand– Are you a parent or a soon-to-be-parent? Commiserate or celebrate parenthood on this channel!

#cooking– Share your favorite recipes or get recommendations on everything from complex cakes to just boiling water. 

#pets– This channel originally came from a member wanting to share pictures of his cats- now it’s a place for foster/adoption notices, cute animal videos, and yes, pictures of members’ pets.

#community-nerds– Are you fascinated by how Indy Hall and other communities function? This is a great place to discuss these things, as well as share articles and resources.

#veg– Are you vegetarian or vegan? Hop into #veg for recipes, restaurant recommendations, and protein sources! (Only mostly joking about that last one)

#bikers– To clarify, this channel is for bicycle riders, not motorcycles (although if you want to talk about that and make a channel, I know there are members that would join you!)- if you ride your bike and want to talk about bikes, trails, and more, this is the channel for you!

#eat-move-lift-heavy– Do you lift, or want to talk about lifting and exercising? This is the place.

#runners–There are a lot of runners at Indy Hall- here you can talk gear, routes, and plan to run together!

#political-chatter-Sometimes politics comes up in the other channels, but this is where you can really dig into it with other members.

#cars-driving-If you love cars, this is the place to share your enthusiasm with other car-lovers!

#coffee– You love it, you need it, it’s Indy Hall’s lifeblood. If you want to talk about hand pouring methods, brewing, roasting, or anything else about coffee, this is the place.

#beer– Do you love beer? This is the channel to share what brews you’ve tried, and what you’re looking forward to!

#science-stuff– This is the channel to share cool science stories- if it fascinates you, we want to hear about it!

Just for kicks

#overheardatthehall– Did you hear something around the Hall that made you giggle, especially taken out of context? Post it here- no context, no attribution allowed!

#gifs– While sometimes we hit a theme, like cats or Bruce Lee, all gifs are welcome!

#photoshop-friday– Every Friday, members hop into this channel to decide what photo from the week we want to tinker with. Once that’s decided, you have until the end of the day to create something amazing and silly with photoshop (or whatever similar program you prefer). At the end of the day, everyone votes for their favorites! Whether you’re new or experienced, all are welcome.

#weirdest-internet– You know those articles or images you stumble across and go, “well, this is super weird”… this is the channel for that!
