It’s Game Time! For the Flyers, and the People at Work Summit!

April 18, 2016 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Hey Hallers,

Last week was a busy one! We met Jeff and re-met Rob, the Spanish-practice group restarted, and we had the pots and pan swap (plus an impromptu Old Fashioned making lesson).

Some of our members also came up with some truly spectacular ideas to further take care of our community you can join in on! First, Troy put together a “Culture Club 2.0” chat, to discuss how we can create more opportunities for members to take care of Indy Hall, and educate each other in how to do that. It’s an ongoing conversation, so hop into to post to get involved!

While that’s about our physical space, Lydia has created a way for remote members, folks who don’t use our space, to connect without having to be here. First she put out a call for remote members to chat, then Max started#remotecoworking in Slack based off of her idea, to connect with other members that virtually cowork. Do you find it hard to make it into Indy Hall, and want to connect with other members in the same virtual boat? Jump in the Slack channel to say hello!

Now, for this week…

This Thursday-Friday is the People at Work Summit!

I’ve been having an amazing time talking to your fellow members, fellow Philadelphians, and folks all over the world planning to speak and participate in this Summit. It’s going to be a wonderful 24 hours of getting into how people work together, and how that’s growing and changing. This is not just about coworking, it’s about everyone in all kinds of industries, ranging from comedy to wrestling to what Neil does. (I’m not sure, but mystery is good, right?)

The best part is that it’s entirely virtual, so you can tune in when you can, and join in a Slack just for participants where we’ve already been having awesome conversations (and Indy Hall members have already been sharing their insights!).

Just like everything at Indy Hall, however, it wouldn’t be the same without you.We have a special discount for Indy Hall members, and will probably have a stream of the Summit at the Hall for you to co-Summit.

Even if you’re NOT attending the summit, we could really use your help! Alex, Adam, and I are going to be busily working on MCing the event on Thursday And Friday of this week – so we won’t have our full attention on Indy Hall like we usually do.

Sean will be here holding down the fort, but we’re looking for a couple of members to help him out by keeping a watchful eye Indy Hall during the day on Thursday and Friday during the day? That could include helping answer questions in #indyhall-ops in Slack, signing for packages, keeping an eye out for folks walking in looking lost, or even giving a tour. Nothing huge, but little things that help make the day run smoothly for everyone!

If you’re able to pitch in, reply to this email and we’ll give you a quick run down of what you might expect and add you to a channel of members who are pitching in for the day so you can help each other out keeping Indy Hall lovely and shiny.

Coming up this week…

Monday, April 18th

Falynn is testing out vegan mac and cheese recipes, and needs you to eat them!She’ll be competing in the best vegan mac and cheese in Philly competition, so will need you to eat the leftovers at lunchtime from her weekend test batches.

Thursday, April 21st

The People at Work Summit begins at noon! See above for how you can join in the fun!

So, it is the third Thursday, but because of the Summit we are pushing Show and Tell back a week to 4/28. Check in next week’s announcements for who our speakers will be!

Friday, April 22nd

If you celebrate Passover, have a wonderful holiday!
