Indy Hall Arts Meeting, Stephan’s Office Hours, and Making Luchador Masks!

January 22, 2016 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Hi Hallers, I hope you weathered the storm well! (is that phrase meant to be a pun? I like it!)

Last week started slow, then got pretty busy by the end! We met new members like Nathan, Milton, Katel, and Tim, and re-met folks we haven’t seen in a while, like Jordan.

Adam also reorganized the mail area!Hopefully this new system will make it a lot easier to find your mail. Check out his post and diagram to make sure you know where to look!

We also had an unbelievable Show and Tell last week- if you were one of our speakers, or took a break from your day to come check it out, thank you! If you missed it, you don’t have to worry! Since we crowdcasted it, you can check out the recording whenever you like.

One of the big things coming up this week is the meeting to plan Spirits and Beasties, the first show in our spring art art season. I grabbed Sean to see what he’s Jammin’ On in his work and with Indy Hall Arts.

 

 

Related to Indy Hall Arts- if you are curious about how to get involved (in any way), check out this list of important dates to know for this season. Also, did you know you can take over the Indy Hall Arts Instagram? 

Happening this week around the Hall…

Tuesday, 1/26

 

Do you like to craft? Now that it’s cold and blustery, it’s a great time to start a project (or learn, if you don’t know how!). Join Amanda Thomas and I on the upstairs couches at 2pm for Full of Craft!

Want to get involved in Indy Hall Arts’s spring season? Come by the meeting at 6:30 to figure out how the first show, Spirits and Beasties, will look!

Sav is a hipster honey!Hipster Honeys is the companion to Hipster Hunks, a feature in Philly Weekly, and Savannah has invited us all to the pre-release party! Hop over to the El Bar at 9pm!

Thursday, 1/28

Stephan is holding office hours! Stephan will be set up in the North conference room from 10am-2pm to offer legal advice on whatever you need, large or small. There are still a few slots available, so sign up! Not sure who Stephan is? Check out his awesome reintroduction.

Saturday, 1/30

It’s Side Project Saturday with Philly Cocoaheads!Come to the Hall to hang out with Tom, Kotaro, and the rest of the Cocoaheads and work on your latest projects in the company of other developers.

Sunday, 1/31

Come make a luchador mask with Ben Levin at Indy Hall! Hop on the post to find out how to sign up, what materials you’ll need, and what to expect!

Coming up later this month…

Sarah and Mikey Prasuhn made it to Philly! Well, they made it a little while ago, but it’s never too late to party! They’re throwing an open house on 2/6- check out the post to RSVP.

An Awesome CPA (who Kate Field recommends) is coming to Indy Hall! On 2/10, he’ll be around the Hall to meet with anyone with questions- check out the post for how to sign up for a time!

Also in case you missed it…

  • Let’s figure out how to connect more remote and new members! Lydia started up a conversation to find more ways to do this- hop on in!
  • Want to come in on the weekends?Longtime member Dave Frigeri works in NYC during the week, but still likes to come into the Hall on weekends. If you want to come in, contact him to see if he’s around!
  • Want to learn how to make things with an Arduino? Josh and Zach want to teach you!

Did I miss something? Do you have questions, comments, or concerns? Come talk to me, or email [email protected]!
