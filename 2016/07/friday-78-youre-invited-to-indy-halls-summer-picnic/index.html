Friday, 7/8- You’re invited to Indy Hall’s Summer Picnic!

July 5, 2016 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Hi Hallers,

Let’s get together and celebrate our Independents*!

This Friday starting at noon, we’re headed over to Spruce Street Harbor Park to eat, drink, hang out, and celebrate! There are lots of food and drink options, roller skating (which is totally optional)… all we need is you! Bring your family and friends and come enjoy a wonderful summer afternoon/evening with us. We’re heading over at noon, but you can join at any time. More information is on the Meetup event– I’ll see youon Friday!

*”Independents” pun courtesy of Halana Dash

What’re you Jammin On?

Last week I sat down to chat with Bob Batz! Bob’s been off traveling in Vienna, so I wanted to find out what he’s been up to since he left. Heads up, this What’re You Jammin’ On is going to make you hungry…

This Week…

Tuesday, 7/5

  • Let’s talk #newhome! Alex will be away, but at 5:30pm Adam and I will be hanging out in the upstairs lounge to chat about any questions or concerns you have about the move, as well asstarting to plan how we’re going to get everything over there!
  • How can vending machines be philanthropic? Meg wants to figure it out! She’s sketched out a few ideas in her post, but needs your help to figure out how exactly it can work. She’s meeting in the kitchen at 6pm-let’s put our brains together!

Friday, 7/8

Let’s party! Come by Spruce Street Harbor Park starting at noon for our (post)4th of July Picnic! There are food and drink options at the park, or you can BYOP (Bring your own picnic).

And don’t miss…

 

  • Mark your calendars for the next blood drive! The next drive Reed is putting together is 8/22 from 9-2.
  • Help kickstart Ben’s hilarious book! Just check out the post, have a giggle, and pitch in a couple bucks if you can. ðŸ™‚
  • Do you have questions about the DNC? Alex put together this helpful post to see how it’ll affect us when it rolls into Philly. (TLDR: It won’t be as bad as the Pope)
  • Finally, SAVE THE DATE! Next Tuesday there are two excellent events going on!
    • At 6:30, Indy Hall Arts is getting together to jam on what’s coming next. All are welcome, whether you’ve been involved with IHArts for ages, or are just curious about how to get involved. We’ll chat about how the spring season went, what we want to do for the fall, and how we can make #newhome our own!
    • Want to give back with your fellow Indy Hallers? A bunch of Indy Hallers are planning to head over to the river cleanup organized by United By Blue from 5-7pm. Check out the post to see how to get involved!
