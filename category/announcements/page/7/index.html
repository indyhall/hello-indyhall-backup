Indy Hall’s First Winter Adventure

January 7, 2015 Alex Hillman Leave a comment

After the success of the first Indy Hall Beach House back in August and September, we’ve had some discussion about what our next adventure should be. And given the snowy season – and the number of Indy Hall members that are into winter sports – we thought that it’d be fun to take a weekend trip to upstate New York!

At this point we’re talking to a few places to figure out prices for everything from accommodations to renting a mini-bus or coach so we don’t have carpool hell + worrying about who gets stuck driving after 2 long days on the mountain.

While we’re figuring out the nitty gritty and the pricing, we wanted to get an idea of what the interested headcount would be. This is important because the more people we get, the better pricing we can get.

Here’s the all-important details that we have so far:

We’re looking at dates at the end of February or beginning of March. On the table are the weekends of:

  • Feb 27th-Mar 1st
  • Mar 13th-15th
  • Mar 20th-22nd

In all cases, we’d plan to leave Philadelphia mid-afternoon on Friday to arrive Friday night, have all day Saturday and Sunday on the mountain, and then leave to return to Philly Sunday late afternoon.

Below is a form that asks for a bit of info from you, and that you’d select which dates COULD work for you. We’ll end up picking the most popular dates and ruling out the ones that don’t work for most people.

Whiteface Mountain has some of the best options in terms of price and availability for us, so that’s where we’re focusing our efforts.

Don’t ski or snowboard? YOU CAN STILL COME!

Whiteface has other activities, including snowtubing and other…less athletic fun things to do in the winter. That includes my personal favorite, which is sitting by the fire and sipping a warm boozy drink ðŸ™‚

Prices – when we have them – will be based on one person per bed, as well as some couples options like sharing a queen or king. If you’re looking to save money and don’t mind sharing a queen or full sized bed, just mention it in the notes when you submit the form below.

MOST IMPORTANTLY we need to have this form filled out by Tuesday, January 13th. Submitting this form DOES NOT commit you to the trip, but be aware that as soon as possible we will be collecting deposits to finalize everything.

Any questions or ideas, don’t hesitate to reach out to [email protected], or jump into the #snowtrip room on Slack ðŸ™‚
