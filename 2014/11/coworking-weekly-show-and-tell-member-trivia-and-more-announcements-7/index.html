“Coworking Weekly,” Show and Tell, Member Trivia and more! Announcements #7!

November 14, 2014 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Are you ready for Indy Hall member trivia?

Click the link below to find out everything going on at Indy Hall this week!

Don’t miss the notes below for more events and details.

#thisweek – highlight reel

Show and Tell

Bring your lunch and your curiosity to this month’s Show and Tell! At noon on Thursday, the 20th:

  • Amanda Thomas will teach us about Feng Shui
  • Michael Norcross will give us a demo on screen printing
  • Eric Steele will teach us about how to make Tom Cruise look tall.

See you in the classroom area on Thursday!

Upcoming Holiday Hours

We will be closed on Thanksgiving and Black Friday, 11/27 and 11/28. Have a lovely little break!

Did you sign up for Indy Hall’s Secret Santa yet?

Sign up for Indy Hall’s Secret Santa! It’s super easy, and sign ups close November 25th. Don’t miss it!

You can subscribe to the Indy Hall events calendar!

All you have to do is go to the Member Calendar, then click this button. Then… ta-da! You’re subscribed!

Have you heard?

Coworking weekly has an amazing podcast! So far there are three episodes for you to enjoy- head here to give your ears a treat! Also, don’t forget to check it out on itunes (and maybe give it a nice rating?)

Member Trivia Question of the Week

Answer correctly, and you win a prize!

Which of our members rode donkeys through Petra (of Indiana Jones fame)?

Send me your guesses at [email protected]!

#thisweek – daybyday

Tuesday

Shop and snack at N3RD MRKT, now 1-6pm for Daylight Savings Time!

Wednesday

  1. Join the B TEAM for a comic book frolic at Noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New World.

  2. Are you full of craft? Come knit, crochet, embroider, draw, or whatever else you’d like to do to unwind! We’ll meet at 2pm at the upstairs couches for about an hour. Want to learn one of these skills? Come on by!

  3. Booze and shmooze at the Maker’s Mixer! Held at the Philadelphia Art Alliance, this event will be a great chance to check out some art and mingle with makers and entrepreneurs from the area. 6:30-9:30 pm, tickets are $5. Check out who will be at the Mixer and what other cool stuff it’ll feature here.

Thursday

  1. Show and Tell! Noon in the classroom area- see above for details.

  2. Interested in writing right? Indy Hall Writer’s Group will be meeting at 12:30 (or after Show and Tell, whichever comes first) at the larger tables upstairs. Talk to Jacki or Erika for more information!

  3. Throw-It-Out Thursday Part Deux! Let’s rock out and declutter! We’ll turn up the music at 5:30, and this time we’ll be able to take all the trash out

  4. Feeling sketchy? Mike Jackson will be checking out Figure Drawing at the Plastic Club this Thursday evening. The model fee is $8, talk to Mike for more details or check out his Groupbuzz post here.

  5. Night Owls, 6-10(ish) pm, will be hosted by the always-wonderful Meg!

Friday

Waffle Day! Have you met Stephan? Did you know what a wonderful person he is? Can you tell he has totally and completely won my heart with waffles? Celebrate the end of the week in the kitchen, 4pm.

#clambake Happy Hour is back with a vengeance!Fancy Friday will be happening at Lucha Cartel, where drinks are only $5 from 4-7pm. We’re heading over at 5pm, see you there!

#comingupsoon

Monday, December 15th

Indy Hall/ National Mechanics Blood Drive will be at National Mechanics. More information is here, or talk to Reed!

That’s all I have for you this week!
