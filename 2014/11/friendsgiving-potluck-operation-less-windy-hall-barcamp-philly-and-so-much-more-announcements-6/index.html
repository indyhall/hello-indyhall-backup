Announcements

Friendsgiving Potluck, Operation “Less Windy Hall,” Barcamp Philly, and so much more! Announcements #6!

November 10, 2014 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Look at all of these great things to do this week!

Click the link below to find out all the awesome things happening around the Hall!

Don’t miss the notes below for more events and details.

#thisweek – highlight reel

Hold on to your butts! We’re introducing Throw It Out Thursday!

We know it gets messy here, and it’s ok! To keep Indy Hall going from “interesting” to a mess, we’re creating Throw It Out Thursday! This Thursday and next at 5:30, we are turning up the music and opening up some wine and beer for a clean up, Indy Hall style!

Interested in how we’re trying to keep you warm this winter?

Check out Operation Less Windy Hall for what we’re up to and how to get involved!

Do you like presents?

Sign up for Indy Hall’s Secret Santa!

Feeling Crafty?

You know Indy Hall is full of creative awesome folks, but have you heard about our two newest resources at Indy Hall? Full of Craft is our crafting group, meeting every Wednesday at 2pm, and Indy Hall Writer’s Workshop is meeting Thursdays at 12:30. More information is below!

Girl Develop It- Indy Hall Friendsgiving is Thursday!

Learn more about the event here, and sign up for what you’re going to bring here!

Vote on my friendsgiving contribution here!

#thisweek – daybyday

Monday

Do you like maps? Want to try making them? Maptime PHL will be meeting from 6:30-9:30 in the classroom area. Bring your laptop- all skill levels are welcome! Talk to Jon Tehrani for more information!

It’s a Monday Night comedy outing!

Join Ellen and Adam for a funcomfortable night seeing Adrienne Truscott’s ASKING FOR IT: A One-Lady Rape About Comedy Starring Her Pussy and Little Else! I never thought I’d see most of those words in the announcements! Tickets are available here.

Tuesday

Shop and snack at N3RD MRKT, now 1-6pm for Daylight Savings Time!

Wednesday

Join the B TEAM for a comic book frolic at Noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New World.

Are you full of craft? Come knit, crochet, embroider, draw, or whatever else you’d like to do to unwind! We’ll meet at 2pm at the upstairs couches every week for about an hour. Want to learn one of these skills? Come on by!

Thursday

  1. Interested in writing right? Indy Hall’s brand new Writer’s Group is having their first meeting 12:30 at the larger tables upstairs. Talk to Jacki or Erika for more information!

  2. Bring your friendship and your food to Girl Develop It and Indy Hall Friendsgiving Potluck, Indy Hall, 6:30pm. Sign up for what you’re bringing, or just drool over what everyone else has RSVPed with here.

  3. Busy publishers Lanternfish Books are partnering with Geekadelphia for a Sherlock-themed show at Geek Night at the Azuka theater. Talk to Amanda or Christine for details.

  4. It’s the penultimate Bond Night! This week we’ll be getting lost in Daniel Craig’s eyes… I mean watching Quantum of Solace at 6pm.

  5. …and don’t forget about Night Owls, 6-10(ish) pm, with the always-lovely Laura Oxenfeld.

Saturday

It’s Barcamp Philly! Philly’s own “unconference,” where participants determine the programming the day of the event! Tickets are still available, and it looks like it’ll be fun!

#comingupsoon

Wednesday, November 19th

Booze and shmooze at the Maker’s Mixer! Held at the Philadelphia Art Alliance, this event will be a great chance to check out some art and mingle with makers and entrepreneurs from the area. 6:30-9:30 pm, tickets are $5.

Monday, December 15th

Indy Hall/ National Mechanics Blood Drive will be at National Mechanics. More information is here, or talk to Reed!
