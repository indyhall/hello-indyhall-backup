What is the reboot?

The Reboot is a three day weekend, (Friday June 5th-Sunday June 7th) where we take apart Indy Hall, clean it, reorganize it, and put it back together.

Why are we doing this?

Indy Hall is a flexible place! It allows you to shake things up so we meet more people, avoid becoming complacent, and adapt the Hall to match the needs of our always-changing membership.

If you’re a newer member (like me!) it also gives you the opportunity to contribute to how your clubhouse is set up, making you feel like a founding member (even if you’ve only been here for a week)!

Will I have to move?

Yup. I will too, as well as Adam and Alex. We won’t know what the layout will be for the desks, but we do want to know what you care about!

By Friday, we will have paper surveys out on all the black desks (and available for Flex members, too!) asking whatÂ qualities you care about. Do you like a tucked-back corner? Do you like being near a window? We want to know!
