Let’s talk about Indy Hall’s future

About 3 weeks ago, Alex posted an extremely important article to his page and to Groupbuzz. If you haven’t read the article about the Future of Indy Hall, please read it now.

It’s finally Reboot time! Here’s what you need to know leading up to this 3 day weekend, in Announcements #35!

June 1, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

It’s finally Reboot Week! Find out everything you need to know to prepare and participate in the video, and don’t miss the notes below for details, links, and lots of other awesome things happening this week!

#highlight-reel

First, something you might’ve missed…

Night Owls is moving to Tuesdays! Members were noticing that it was getting tougher to carve out time on Thursdays, so thought Tuesdays were worth a try!

There will not be Night Owls THIS THURSDAY in preparation for the Reboot.

We are also still looking for another Night Owls host! There are a lot of great reasons why this is a great position- you have a set time to work cut into your schedule, you get to meet a lot of members you might not see often or during the day, and you get to work with the Indy Hall team on accomplishing whatever goals you want to set out for yourself during Night Owls! For more information you can come talk to us or email [email protected], and of course, Magnificent Meg Widholm is a great resource as well.

Here’s what to expect for the Reboot…

The basics are this:

1 – Indy Hall will be “closed” for normal operations from 6pm on thursday until 9am on Monday morning.

If you can’t make it to Reboot but are able to help us get prepared for Thursday, come talk to myself, @Adam Teterus or @Sam Abrams so we can help you find a small project to help out with (like building boxes, decluttering…)

2 – During the Reboot weekend itself, we’ll be cleaning and improving Indy Hall and need as much help as we can get! This is a great way to find something you want to make better at Indy Hall, leave your mark, and get to know some other members along the way.

If you can sign up to help out, even if it’s just for a few hours, please add your name to “The Players” section of the Playbook.

3 – At the end of the weekend, all full time members will find new places to sit, and perhaps more importantly, new people to sit with.

If you’re a full-time member, we have a packet of more information for you (to be handed out tomorrow), and you can check out the online version here.

So what can YOU do RIGHT NOW?

  • Ask questions. The #1 challenge with the Reboot is making sure that people know what to expect, because there are some inherent parts to the process that can’t be predicted (like the exact layout, or where people will sit). If you have ANY questions or concerns, please ask them on the Groupbuzz post, come talk to one of us, or email us!
  • Talk to your Indy Hall neighbors & coworkers. Make sure that everyone you talk to knows about this thread. Seriously, this is one of the most valuable things you can do and it’s SUPER simple.
  • Keep an eye on the Reboot playbook! 
    • If you can help out, a lot or a little, sign up under “The Players” section!
    • Add projects/improvements that YOU would like to see & be a part of (even if you’re not sure how to do it or aren’t able to “lead” it). Not everything will get done, but the more help, the better!

Thank you for all your help so far, and I look forward to getting messy, doing projects, and making this place even more beautiful with you!
