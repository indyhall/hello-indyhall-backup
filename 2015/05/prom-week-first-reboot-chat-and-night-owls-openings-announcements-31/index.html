Prom Week, First Reboot Chat, and Night Owls Openings! Announcements #31!

May 4, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Hey, will you go to Prom with me?

N3RD St Prom is this weekend! Find out what fun pre-prom things will be happening, what we’re doing to start prepping for reboot, and more in the video!

Don’t miss all the notes below for a lot more happening this week, like Phil Ives’s Lunch and Learn, a Bad Kitten improv show, and more!

#highlight-reel

It’s Prom Week!

It’s finally here! 80s Prom is this Saturday! Join us from 8-11:30pm at the Painted Bride for a dance party with a photobooth, DJ Haveboard, and lazers…(maybe?). The first two for sure, plus great drinks and fantastic prom-wear.

Have you bought tickets yet? 

There are still tickets available, and you can enter our Instagram contest to win 2 tickets! Simply post your prom photos (or anything 80s related, if you don’t have prom pics handy), and make sure to hashtag them with #n3rdstprom! On Tuesday, we’ll draw a winner!

I can’t wait to see you all at the prom. It’s going to be a fantastic night!

Let’s Talk Reboot!

Our last open State of the Hall talk was a great success! I loved getting your input on what works and what doesn’t with communication in Indy Hall. You all are so articulate and responsive when it comes to making this place run smoothly, and it’s one of the things I love most about Indy Hall. Thank you!

So, we decided this was a great way to start to talk about the reboot, which is in a month! Time flies when you’re coworking, huh?

This Friday, May 8th, at 2pm, we’ll meet in the classroom to talk about how we’re organizing Reboot, what you’d like to see, and what worked (and didn’t) from last year.  Join us!

Interested in becoming a Night Owl?

If you missed it last week on Groupbuzz, our delightful ray of sunshine, Shayna, has decided to step down from running Night Owls. She promised to keep in touch, so we’ll still hear from her wonderful, bubbly self.

Meg will still be hosting Night Owls, and you (yes, you!) can join her as host!

Night Owls is an excellent platform for helping someone who has a day job or other daytime commitments get more connected to the community and spend more time at Indy Hall with other members. There are lots of other great reasons to be a Night Owls host, all available here in the Groupbuzz post. 

If you are interested in hosting, or just want to talk about it more, come chat with us or email [email protected] It’s a lot of fun!

#thisweek – daybyday

Monday

Need a moment? Come meditate with us, and take 15 moments! 1:30 in the North conference room.

Tuesday

Join Phil Ives for a Userkit Lunch and Learn! Not sure what Userkit is? Join Phil for some learning and a (free!) lunch- all you have to do is RSVP!

Full of Craft is now on Tuesdays! Have you thought about learning to knit, crochet, or needlepoint, but haven’t had the time or the teacher? Join us in the upstairs lounge for ten minutes or an hour for some creating and some chatting. We like you. 2pm every Tuesday.

Wednesday

  1. Join the B-TEAM for a comic book frolic at noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New Worlds.
  2. More meditation! 
  3. Cheer on the N3RD St Nerds in their kickball game! Check the #kickbawl channel for game times, every Wednesday!
  4. Remember the days in September, when delightful improv group Bad Kitten performed every Wednesday? Well they’re back for an encore Wednesday show at 7:30 at Philly Improv Theater! Tickets are available online, and it’s BYOB!

Thursday

  1. Shhhh! Meditation time.
  2. Night Owls, 6-10(ish) pm, hosted by lovely Meg!

Saturday

Save the date, and buy some hairspray- Indy Hall is putting on an 80s Prom! Just picture it- pre-spiked punch, dancing like goofballs to your favorite 80s hits, and wearing the best/worst prom gear you can find! How can you miss it?

Buy your tickets today!!

#comingupsoon

Throughout May

Have we talked about improv? Have you figured, “Yeah, I’ll come see one of Sam’s shows!” Well, now there are a lot to choose from! Check out my google calendar roundup of show dates, and if you want, you can use my discount code! When you’re buying tickets, put in the code “teachersam,” for 30% off!

Monday, May 11th

It’s the first ever Wine and Code Night! After learning to write code, awesome member Constance Ip realized it was frustrating to try and write alone- so she did what Indy Hallers do best and planned some coworking! Join Constance for some wine and pizza, and start a mini-hackathon!

Thursday, May 14th

Join Lansie for her design agency’s 4th birthday party! It’s themed “Four More Years,” and will feature free beer and an election for their next puppy mascot! RSVP here, and don’t miss it!

Friday, May 29th

Come check out the first comedy group comprised of people on the autism spectrum with Asperger’s Are Us  at Drexel University from 6-8pm. Tickets are free, but you can RSVP here!

Reboot approaches!

Reboot will be June 5-7, the first Friday, Saturday, and Sunday in June. There’s a lot to plan, but it’s also going to be a lot of fun! Stay tuned to the announcements for Reboot info, and put your ideas in our trello for what you’d like to do!

Did I miss anything? Do you have any questions, comments, or concerns? Email me at [email protected]!
