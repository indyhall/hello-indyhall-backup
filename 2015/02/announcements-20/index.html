Scotch Night is finally here! Plus tons of fun events every day, in Announcements #20!

February 16, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

It’s the 20th Announcements Post, and it’s FULL. 

like, incredibly full. Fun things every day. We’re so lucky!

Or you can use this link.

Please, take a look at the notes below- I don’t want you to miss out on any of the great events happening this week!

#thisweek – Highlight Reel

Beam me up, Scotty!

Scotch Night is finally here! This Friday, we’ll explore the Highlands and the Lowlands of Scotland, through delicious scotch and delectable treats. Still haven’t RSVPed? You still can here! Want to contribute? We could still use contributions to cover shipping costs, and of more Scottish snacks.

Next Wednesday is Town Hall!

Don’t forget! Town Hall is Wednesday, 2/25 at 5pm at the Painted Bride.

Join us to talk about how we’re doing, and where we’re going as a community. Like a “State of the Hall.” If you’re lucky, Adam and I will sit in the back and do our proud-Biden impressions. Maybe. It involves a lot of pointing, and probably some winking.

Everything you need to know about Town Hall is in our post here, and the invitation to RSVP is here.

Ready to be inspired?

I know I sure am, because Show and Tell is this Thursday!

This month, we’re going to learn from:

  1. Matt Calhoun about Alan Turing’s Machines
  2. Will Stallwood on Splice, his new game available on major game systems
  3. Tom Piarulli on CocoaHeads, and upcoming Indy Hall Hackdays

Join us at noon in the classroom! 

Winter Trip still has spots available!

Interested in having a winter adventure with your Indy Hall buddies? Sign up today for the winter trip! There are still spots available, and everyone is welcome!

#thisweek – daybyday

Monday

Join Adam and other coworking folks in Philly for Happy Hour! We used to get together with other coworking folks for happy hours, and that’s kind of… fallen off. So super-man-thing Adam has put together a happy hour at Victoria Freehouse, 5pm. Join us!

Interested in playing Super Smash Bros with your Indy Hall Bros? Our video game guru Jonathan Pickard is starting a Smash Bros group on Monday Nights from 5-8pm! If you’re interested, reply to his groupbuzz post that you’d like to participate, or go talk to him! (He sits right across from Alex).

Tuesday

Meditation Time is now twice a week! If your Thursdays get too hectic, like mine do, and you forget to go meditate, now you have two chances! Join us in North conference room at 1:30, every Tuesday and Thursday!

Wednesday

  1. Join the B-TEAM for a comic book frolic at noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New Worlds.
  2.  Are you full of craft? Come knit, crochet, embroider, draw, or whatever else you’d like to do to unwind! We’ll meet at 2pm at the upstairs couches for about an hour. Want to learn one of these skills? Come on by!
  3. Instacart is having their one-year Indyversary! Celebrate with them in the kitchen at 5pm- There will be treats and shots, with George Shotz! (see what I did there?)

Thursday

  1. Interested in writing right? Indy Hall Writer’s Group will be meeting at 12:30 (or after Show and Tell, whichever comes first) at the larger tables upstairs. Talk to Erika for more information!
  2. Shhhh! Meditation time is at 1:30 in the North conference room.
  3. It’s time again for Show and Tell! Check out above for who’s speaking, and join us! Noon in the classroom area.
  4. Want to put the FUN in Fundraising? Or, at least, learn how to do it? Awesome member Lansie Sylvia has put together an all-star panel to learn from! There are only 45 spots available, so sign up now to bask in their glorious knowledge!
  5. Do you miss seeing Mohan around? I do. Our happy-hour-loving buddy is going to be at Barcade for their Midwest Beer Night, starting at 4pm. (He’ll be there around 4:45).
  6. Go catch this awesome theater/dance piece with Ellen Chenoweth! She’s seeing “The Vulgar Early Works” at Fringe Arts 2/19 at 7pm. All the details and ticket information is in her groupbuzz post. Don’t miss it! She says these performers are being compared to Shakespeare. What!? Awesome!
  7. Night Owls, 6-10(ish) pm, hosted by lovely Meg!

Friday

It’s finally here! It’s finally Scotch Night! You can still RSVP using the anyvite, and contribute with Scottish snacks or toward shipping costs. Talk to James Falconi, Adam, or myself for more details.

#comingupsoon

Wednesday, February 25th

It’s Town Hall! Join us at the Painted Bride from 5-7pm.

Friday, March 13th- Sunday, March 15th

It’s the Winter Trip! There are still a few spots open, so sign up here!
