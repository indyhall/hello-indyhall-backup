Last Chance for Food Drive, Holiday Parties, and a Co-op Lunch and Learn!

November 25, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Hi Hallers! I hope you had a fun, restful, food-filled Thanksgiving!

Now that that holiday is over, there’s a flurry of events leading up to the next one.

It’s your last chance to contribute to the Food Drive (and help us beat Weblinc!

On Wednesday, 12/2 we’re taking the food to be weighed… and Hallers, apparently Weblinc is kicking our butts at this food drive. Let’s come from behind for a rousing win!

Adam took over the video for this week to let you know everything you need to know about the Food Drive, like how you can contribute, the benefits of donating, and where his donated cans came from.

Also to get in the holiday spirit, the parties are starting to roll in! Amy Hoy and Lauren Ancona are having an art show/holiday party where you can buy Lauren’s gorgeous map blueprints; Reed posted about a party we’re all invited to; and Indy Hall is having our annual potluck holiday party! 

We’re also getting in the spirit (and decorating for the party) on December 10th at 5pm! If making decorations isn’t your thing, you can also add to the the holiday playlist Adam started up!

Happening this week around the Hall…

Tuesday, 12/1

Curious about co-ops? A little while ago, the subject of co-ops and how they work came up in Slack. Max, one of our remote members, runs a food co-op in Elkins Park, so will be leading a Lunch and Learn at noon about co-ops, how they work, and how they build community! 

This is also the first time we’re using a new tool that allows everyone to participate in discussions, whether you can be physically present or not! RSVP on the post, and join the conversation!

LETS WATCH STAR WARS! 

Join us at 6pm in the classroom to watch the original trilogy, starting this Tuesday!

Wednesday, 12/2

It’s the next Video Vault with Ellen Chenoweth! This week, join her and artists from Headlong to watch Lela Aisha Jones’ Native Portals: Lynching and Love. Lela herself will be around to talk about the piece and answer questions! Upstairs lounge, 6:30pm.

Thursday, 12/3

Abby has invited you to the Digital Project Manager Beer Swap! Join Abby and the other folks of DPM Philly at 6pm at O3, or check out the post for their 2016 meetups!

Friday, 12/4

It’s not too late to go to Wordcamp US! This Friday and Saturday is the national WordPress convention, and tickets are still available! Talk to Reed or check out the post for more information!

Come see Bad Kitten at Kung Fu Necktie at 8pm! For just $5, you see a bunch of comedy acts, including Neil’s and my improv team. After the show is a DJed dance party!

 

A few more things…

  • Have you listened to Dave Speers’s podcast? It’s a personal look at real estate and Philly history- check out the latest episode, about how Standard Tap and Johnny Brenda’s started out!
  • Do you look at the Kinshop in the gallery and think, “I want to make cool stuff!” I know I do! Sean is planning a few workshops and collaborative creating days- check out the post to talk about what you’d like to learn or teach!

Did I miss something? Do you have questions, comments, or concerns? Come talk to me, or email [email protected]!
