The Scotch Order Deadline, New Members, and Tons of Events! Don’t Miss Announcements #18!

January 29, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Are you in winter hibernation? Get outta there! There’s great stuff going on at Indy Hall!

Click the image below to find out what’s happening!

Or use this link.

#thisweek – Highlight Reel

Save the Date! It’s Town Hall Time!

Indy Hall will be having its first Town Hall of the year on February 25th at 5 pm, at the Painted Bride. Alex will put up a separate post soon with more information. Don’t miss it!

THIS FRIDAY is the last day to order your bottle for Scotch Night!

Listen, Indy Hall. You know you want to go to Scotch Night. So order your bottle today. Order through the anyvite invitation, and then pay for the bottle you’re sponsoring on Venmo at [email protected], or by cash or check to Adam, Alex or I!

Look at all our new members introducing themselves!

We have a bevy of awesome new folks coming into the hall, and there are lots of great intros to check out on Groupbuzz!

Whether you’re new, or whether you’ve been around for a while (but not recently, like Maddie,) an introduction allows our members to get to know you better. If you’re a member that’s been around, it gives you a great way to get to know all these new faces beyond just “oh yeah, that’s the guy that sits by the window.” So check it out! Get to know  Jim, Dan, Christine, Savannah, Mandy, Matt, and all the other great new members!

There’s so much to do at Indy Hall!

This week alone, we have Bollywood Night, the Best of 2014 Listening Party, Art Shows, Improv and Comedy Shows, and a DJ set by Finn.

Don’t miss the notes below. There are so many awesome things to do this week!

#thisweek – daybyday

Tuesday

It’s Bollywood Night! Whether you’re a first time Bollywood viewer or an old SRK devotee, 3 Idiots is a super accessible, delightfully silly movie, that you can see right here at Indy Hall! Meet in the classroom at 7pm.

Wednesday

  1. Join the B-TEAM for a comic book frolic at noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New Worlds.
  2.  Are you full of craft? Come knit, crochet, embroider, draw, or whatever else you’d like to do to unwind! We’ll meet at 2pm at the upstairs couches for about an hour. Want to learn one of these skills? Come on by!

Thursday

  1. Interested in writing right? Indy Hall Writer’s Group will be meeting at 12:30 (or after Show and Tell, whichever comes first) at the larger tables upstairs. Talk to Jacki or Erika for more information!
  2. Shhhh! Meditation time is at 1:30 in the North conference room.
  3. Night Owls, 6-10(ish) pm, hosted by lovely Laura!
  4. It’s the Best of 2014 Listening Party! Adam and Donnie have curated some albums to listen to and discuss together at Indy Hall!

Friday

This is the deadline for ordering your scotch for Scotch night! Order through the anyvite invitation, and then pay for the bottle you’re sponsoring on Venmo at [email protected], or by cash or check to Adam, Alex or I! More details are on Groupbuzz, or contact [email protected] with other questions.

Art you wondering what to do Friday night? How about the Fun-a-Day art show? Artists (including lovely member Sonia Petruse) have crafted a piece of art every day this month, and this Friday and Saturday you can check out what they’ve created! 7-11pm at 34 Yoga in West Philly.

Ready to laugh? Come check out Bad Kitten at l’etage for First Friday, at 7:30 pm. Then, head on over to PHIT to see Eric Steele and I premiere our sketch duo, Just a Couple of Uncles, at Theme Show at 9pm!

Saturday

Get yer dance on! Badass member Finn has a DJ set at Bottle Bar from 10pm-2am. AWESOME!

Sunday

Can’t get enough of Bad Kitten? Neither can we! We love…us! If you missed our Friday show, or just want to double up on your Kittens this weekend, come see us at PHIT at 7pm.

#comingupsoon

Monday, February 9th

Interested in playing Super Smash Bros with your Indy Hall Bros? Our video game guru Jonathan Pickard is looking to start a Smash Bros group on Monday Nights! If you’re interested, reply to his groupbuzz post that you’d like to participate!

Thursday, February 19th

Want to put the FUN in Fundraising? Or, at least, learn how to do it? Awesome member Lansie Sylvia has put together an all-star panel to learn from! There are only 45 spots available, so sign up now to bask in their glorious knowledge!

Friday, February 20th

Scotch Night has returned! This year we’ll be exploring the Highlands and the Lowlands, with many scotches that are not available in the US. To participate, RSVP and sign up for which Scotch you’d like to order here.

Do you have questions, comments, concerns, or anything I missed? Email me at [email protected]!
