Labor Day, Financial Workshop, and more! Announcements #49!

September 7, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

You better work!

It’s Labor Day, and Indy Hall is a little… empty without you! Check out the video and the post for what’s coming up once everyone is back from the three day weekend!

Don’t miss the notes below for everything going on this week!

#highlight-reel

Money Money Money Money… Moneyyyyy

Join Finn for a financial workshop at Indy Hall this Tuesday, 9/8 at 6pm in the classroom.

One of his friends will come in and talk for about 30 minutes about saving and personal finance, and, if you like, will set up one on one meetings. All for free. Don’t miss it.

Indyskillz is killing it!

If you haven’t checked out Indyskillz in a while, you’re missing some great skill sharing going on! Check out Lydia’s post about what everyone has learned from each other this month, and hop on the Groupbuzz post or the #indyskillz channel in slack to see what you can learn or teach today!

Not sure what I’m talking about? Indyskillz is Indy Hall’s skill swap- how it works is you share three things you’d like to teach, and three things you’d like to learn, and members let you know if they want to teach or learn those things with you! Everything goes on the website, so you can look up who can teach you, or who might want to learn from you.

This is also put together by awesome remote member Lydia, so if you learn something cool, make sure to shoot her a message about it (and a picture, if possible!)- you’ll end up on next month’s roundup!

Afflictions is having a party!

Remember last week when I said Lanternfish is launching the Afflictions audiobook? Well, they’re having a launch party!

Join Amanda, Christine, and the other fantastic people that worked on the Audiobook for readings and literary-themed drink specials at Tattooed Mom, Tuesday 9/15 at 8pm.

  2. More meditation!

LET’S CLEAN OUT THE FRIDGE! Oh man, that fridge. It already needs more TLC (both the music and Tender Loving Care), so join Adam and I in cleaning that puppy out at 4pm. Maybe we’ll play TLC while we scrub. I don’t want no scrubs, but that fridge needs some scrubs.

#coming up

Thursday, September 10th, and Thursday, September 17th

You know Savannah, right? You know her, you love her, you maybe have gone to Square One with her… well, Savannah’s up to something big!

She is choreographing a 5 person, 50-minute piece for Fringe! It’s a huge undertaking, so let’s go support her! Both shows are at 8pm at the Glass Factory, and all the information you need to buy tickets is in her groupbuzz post.

I’ll see you there!

September 19th

PodCamp and ContentCamp are coming to the University of Sciences! If you’re not familiar, ContentCamp is an unconference about the future of content, and PodCamp is an unconference about anything and everything digital and social media.

This year they’ve joined forces for a truly stupendous unconference! Pop over to Reed’s post for all the details!

September 24th

Laugh away your pre-Pope worries with Untitled at Indy Hall! Untitled is my improv group that does improvised shows based off of an artist’s work. We’ve worked with Sean and Michael Norcross, and now we’re bringing the funny to you!

Join us in the gallery at 6pm for a Happy Hour and at 7pm for the show! The improvisers will be working off of Kin, the group show. It’s going to be a blast, so don’t miss it.

October 9th

Join Hila for Red Sofa Salon’s 2 year anniversary!

There will be readings by poets Rich Villar, J. Mae Barizo, and Yesenia Montilla, cupcakes, a DJ dance party… all here in the gallery! You don’t want to miss it. All the details are in the event– tix are $7 in advance, $10 at the door.

October 23rd

Are you sorely missing Scotch Night? Wishing it’d come around more than once a year? Well, you’re not alone.

Introducing American Whiskey Night! RSVP here, then sign up to contribute a bottle, or a quintessentially American snack!

Please RSVP by October 2nd! This is going to be a sloppy good time.

December 4th-6th

Wordcamp US is coming to Philly! Check out Reed’s post for all the details, and get excited!
