Untitled Comes to Indy Hall, Happy Hour, and Popeapalooza! Announcements #51!

September 21, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

Get ready for Popeapalooza! 

Check out the video for what you need to know about Indy Hall on Pope week, as well as what else is coming up!

Don’t miss the notes in the post for what to look forward to in October, as well as what is up this week.

#highlight-reel

Let’s have a pre-Pope Happy Hour and Show!

I know, the Pope is coming this week. I’ll get to that in a minute. 

First! For the first time ever, Untitled, one of my improv groups, is coming to Indy Hall to do a show! Plus, beforehand, we’ll have a happy hour, because with all the transportation silliness, we’ll need a drink with friends.

So what does that mean for you? If you want to join us, and I hope you do, you can come grab a drink with your Indy Hall friends at 6pm down in the gallery, and at 7pm we’ll start the show!

My improv group and I will talk to a few of the artists about the work in KIN, and we’ll perform a 45-minute show completely made up on the spot. You’ll laugh, you’ll have a great time, and no one will make you go onstage (don’t worry)! If you’ve ever said, “yeah, I’ve wanted to see Sam do improv but not have to find where she performs,” you’re in luck! All of the details are in this post, or you can check the meetup!

Popeapocalypse is nigh!

You excited? You stressed? Somewhere in between? We’re here for you.

Indy Hall will be OPEN all week.That’s right- if you can get here, we’re here.

Here’s what is important to know….

  • We want to make SURE that the doors get locked at night, especially near the end of the week. All these people are going to need a bathroom, and we don’t need it to be here.
  • There’s no trash pickup this week. If you were yearning for helping with the trash this week, I’m sorry. What you can do is make sure to break down your boxes, and be ready to give us a hand next week!
  • We love you. It’s going to be fine.

#thisweek – daybyday

Tuesday

  1. Full of Craft is on Tuesdays! Have you thought about learning to knit, crochet, or needlepoint, but haven’t had the time or the teacher? Join us in the upstairs lounge for ten minutes or an hour for some creating and some chatting. We like you. 2pm every Tuesday.
  2. It’s Tuesday Night Owls! Join Meg and Kelsey for some meditation and evening coworking from 6-10pm!

Wednesday

  1. Join the B-TEAM for a comic book frolic at noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New Worlds. Check the #bteam channel on slack for exact times and great chatter about comics and comic book-related stuff!
  2. More meditation! 
  3. N3RD Market is back! Get your fruit and veg on down the street by Christ Church, from 2-7pm.

Thursday

Shhhh! Meditation time is at 1:30.

Laugh away your pre-Pope worries with Untitled at Indy Hall! Untitled is my improv group that does improvised shows based off of an artist’s work. We’ve worked with Sean and Michael Norcross, and now we’re bringing the funny to you!

Join us in the gallery at 6pm for a Happy Hour and at 7pm for the show! The improvisers will be working off of Kin, the group show. It’s going to be a blast, so don’t miss it.

#Coming-soon

October 2nd

Get ready for Cipher Prime taking over first Friday! Stay tuned to the announcements to find out what to expect for the big opening Friday! Also, who got to see their tech at Show and Tell? Was it awesome? There’s gonna be more of that.

October 8th

Want to watch an award-winning documentary here at Indy Hall? How about talk to the filmmakers?

Yeah, I want to do that too! Join us for a screening of “I am Santa Claus” and a Q&A with the filmmakers at 6:30pm here at Indy Hall.

Don’t forget to RSVP! The first few people to RSVP will get to submit a question to wrestling Superstar Mick Foley, who will answer them in a recorded video just for us. Oh heck yes.

October 9th

Join Hila for Red Sofa Salon’s 2 year anniversary!

There will be readings by poets Rich Villar, J. Mae Barizo, and Yesenia Montilla, cupcakes, a DJ dance party… all here in the gallery! You don’t want to miss it. All the details are in the event– tix are $7 in advance, $10 at the door.

October 23rd

Are you sorely missing Scotch Night? Wishing it’d come around more than once a year? Well, you’re not alone.

Introducing American Whiskey Night! RSVP here, then sign up to contribute a bottle, or a quintessentially American snack!

Please RSVP by October 2nd! This is going to be a sloppy good time.

December 4th-6th

Wordcamp US is coming to Philly! Check out Reed’s post for all the details, and get excited!

Did I miss anything? Do you have questions, comments or concerns? Email me at [email protected], or come talk to me in person!
