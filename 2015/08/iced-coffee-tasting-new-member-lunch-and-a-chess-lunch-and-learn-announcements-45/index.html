Iced Coffee Tasting, New Member Lunch, and A Chess Lunch and Learn! Announcements #45!

August 7, 2015 Sam Abrams Leave a comment

We have so many member-driven events this week!

Check out the video to see what’s going on at the Hall, especially on Wednesday. Keep an eye out for another background cameo, too!

Don’t miss the notes below for details and more events going on this week!

Super thanks to Neil, Eric, James Falconi, Nathan, and Thomas! You’re fantastic!

#highlight-reel

Get your coffee fix! (and learn about it, too!)

Coffee fans Nathan Johnson and James Falconi have set up a special iced coffee demonstration with Greenstreet coffee this Wednesday, 8/12 at 12:30. Join us outside of the South conference room (by the kitchen) for homebrew methods, a way to do large batch brewing here at the hall, and a keg of iced coffee. 

You read that right. It’s going to be delicious… don’t miss it!

Get to know our new members!

You know from Groupbuzz and from being around the Hall that there are a bunch of new members… now, you have a designated chance to get to know each other!

Dynamic duo Neil Bardhan and Eric Steele have put together a New Member Lunch this Wednesday in the kitchen at noon! It’s BYOLunch, so come by and say hello! (and stick around for the coffee tasting!)

Want to learn more about chess?

Remember Thomas Thomas’s great Show and Tell about chess?

If you don’t, here’s the skinny- Thomas is a pretty awesome chess player, and if you have ever played him, you know he knows his stuff.

This Friday, Thomas wants to share his knowledge with you! Join him at noon in the classroom for a Lunch and Learn about Endgames!

Whether you play a lot, or barely at all, I guarantee you’ll learn something. If there’s something specific you’d like to learn, hop on the Groupbuzz post and just ask!

See you Friday!

#thisweek – daybyday

Tuesday

  1. Full of Craft is on Tuesdays! Have you thought about learning to knit, crochet, or needlepoint, but haven’t had the time or the teacher? Join us in the upstairs lounge for ten minutes or an hour for some creating and some chatting. We like you. 2pm every Tuesday.
  2. No Indy Hall Arts Meeting this week! Panels are at the Hall, and all the info you need to get creating is here! 
  3. It’s Tuesday Night Owls! Join Meg for some meditation and evening coworking from 6-10pm!

Wednesday

  1. Join the B-TEAM for a comic book frolic at noon, gathering in the gallery to walk to Brave New Worlds. Check the #bteam channel on slack for exact times and great chatter about comics and comic book-related stuff!
  2. More meditation! 
  3. N3RD Market is back! Get your fruit and veg on down the street by Christ Church, from 2-7pm.
  4. Join us for a new member lunch at noon! Bring your lunch and say hello!
  5. Stick around for an iced coffee demo at 12:30 outside of South conference room! Greenstreet coffee is coming to show us a few ways to make the best iced coffee, and they’re bringing an iced coffee keg. yep.

Thursday

Shhhh! Meditation time is at 1:30.

Friday

Join Thomas for a chess Lunch and Learn! Thomas is a dedicated chess player, and is going to share some of his knowledge with you! Join him at noon in the classroom!

#coming up

Monday, August 24th

The next Blood Drive is the 20th Indy Hall/National Mechanics Blood Drive! For the 20th drive, Reed is shooting his sights higher, getting as many N3RD street tech businesses as we can to join us, and we’re raising our goal to 40!

Devnuts is on board, and we’ll have two locations for the drive – our familiar National Mechanics and also Devnuts, at 908 North 3rd. To sign up for a time slot, head over to the site and type in “Indy Hall.”

Thursday, September 10th, and Thursday, September 17th

You know Savannah, right? You know her, you love her, you maybe have gone to Square One with her… well, Savannah’s up to something big!

She is choreographing a 5 person, 50-minute piece for Fringe! It’s a huge undertaking, so let’s go support her! Both shows are at 8pm at the Glass Factory, and all the information you need to buy tickets is in her groupbuzz post.

I’ll see you there!

December 4th-6th

Wordcamp US is coming to Philly! Check out Reed’s post for all the details, and get excited!

Did I miss anything? Do you have questions, comments or concerns? Email me at [email protected], or come talk to me in person!
