INDYSKILLZ

Indyskillz is an on-going initiative that helps match Indy Hallers who have something to teach with those who have something to learn! Each month we encourage you to post something you’re interested in learning and something you’re happy to teach. If you see someone else post a match, reach out (over email, Slack, or in person) and find a time to start sharing!
