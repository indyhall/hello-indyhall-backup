Closing down Indy Hall for the Night!

So, you’ve become a keyholder! Congratulations! 

I’m sure that Adam or Sam have given you a tour, but a refresher never hurts, right? Awesome member Annie took some pictures during her keyholder tour to help you out!

Here are all the spots to hit while closing down Indy Hall:

  1. Start in the kitchen…

2. Take a walk around the upstairs.

Head towards the hammock/elevator lobby…

Then on the North side…

Head towards the North conference room…

Then towards the South conference room…

Check if there are any space heaters running in the conference rooms, then head downstairs!

and, the most important thing…

Don’t forget to deadbolt the door when you leave!
